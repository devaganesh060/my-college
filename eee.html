EEE

The DEEE (Electrical and Electronics Engineering) branch is a diverse field that encompasses the study and application of electrical and electronic principles. Students in DEEE learn about power systems, control systems, electronics, and electrical machinery. This branch equips individuals with the knowledge and skills to design, analyze, and maintain electrical systems. DEEE professionals play a crucial role in various industries, including power generation, automation, and telecommunications. With a focus on both theoretical and practical aspects, DEEE prepares graduates to contribute to the development and advancement of electrical and electronic technologies in a wide range of applications.
