MECH(DMEC)

Mechanical Engineering is a broad field that involves the design, analysis, and manufacturing of mechanical systems and devices. It encompasses the study of thermodynamics, fluid mechanics, materials science, and mechanical design. Mechanical engineers are trained to solve complex engineering problems related to energy, motion, and force. The branch plays a key role in industries such as automotive, aerospace, energy, and manufacturing. Mechanical engineering is dynamic, continually evolving to incorporate advancements in technology and sustainable practices, making it a cornerstone discipline in modern engineering.
