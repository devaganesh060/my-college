CIVIL(DCE)

Civil Engineering is a discipline that involves the planning, design, construction, and maintenance of infrastructure projects, including bridges, roads, buildings, and water systems. Civil engineers play a crucial role in shaping the physical environment, addressing issues related to urbanization, transportation, and environmental sustainability. This branch encompasses various sub-disciplines such as structural, geotechnical, transportation, and environmental engineering. Civil engineers collaborate with architects, urban planners, and environmental scientists to create resilient and sustainable solutions. With a focus on public safety and infrastructure development, civil engineering remains integral to the progress of society.
